VideoMCC

a New Benchmark for Video Comprehension

Dataset description

To assess models trained for the VideoMCC task we created a large-scale dataset comparable in size with largest available datasets. The VideoMCC dataset was created from TV News Show videos donated by Internet Archive using a semi-automatic procedure described in details in the paper.

The VideoMCC dataset is available for free to research groups, individual researches and non-profit organisations for research purposes. For commercial use please contact the Archive at info@archive.org.

VideoMCC dataset description:

  • Training set: 191028 multiple-choice questions
  • Testing set: 81476 multiple-choice questions
  • Video files: 272504 video files in AVI format (resolution is 171x128). One video corresponds to one multiple-choice question.
  • Captions files: 324952 and 194946 sentences for training and testing sets respectively.
